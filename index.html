About

Hi! I’m Rehmat, a Mathematics & Computer Science student fascinated by the idea of building things that think in more than one language. I’ve explored machine learning across various domains: public policy, bioacoustics, weather forecasting, and medical imaging, but my primary interest lies in multilingual NLP and machine translation, especially for low-resource languages. I am currently interested in exploring: -Culturally aware and linguistically inclusive multilingual NLP -Discourse sensitive and context aware machine translation -Studying social behavior and societal trends through large-scale language data
